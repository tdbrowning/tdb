RESEARCH Tim Browning - professor in number theory

Outline

  • I run a research group at IST Austria, where I work at the interface of analytic number theory and Diophantine geometry. I organise an Algebraic Geometry & Number Theory Seminar with Tamas Hausel and a Working Seminar.

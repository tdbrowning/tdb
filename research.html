RESEARCH Tim Browning - professor in number theory

Outline

  • I run a research group at IST Austria, where I work at the interface of analytic number theory and Diophantine geometry. I organise the:
    • ✑ Mathematics Colloquium with Jan Maas
    • ✑ Algebraic Geometry & Number Theory Seminar with Tamas Hausel
    • ✑ Browning group working seminar
